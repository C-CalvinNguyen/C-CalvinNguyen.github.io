About Me

Bio

Calvin spent some time as a bartender going about life chatting to co-workers where everyday was "just another day" however he decided this loop would not continue, and so followed his passions of programming and arts where he was able to create something with his own two hands. He now attends George Brown College in the Computer Programmer Analyst (T127/T177) program where he has learned more in-depth about programming, web development, app development, and hopes to create greater things while working with like-minded individuals on this new path.


Career Goals

Throughout my life there have been few things which I have truly felt passionate enough to make a career from, those being the arts and computer programming, but what compelled me to these vastly different fields? Programming and technology as a whole have been an integral part of modern society and is constantly evolving as I have witnessed myself through the introduction of things like the iPod, smartphones, and social media platforms. As technology has had an impact on my life, I too wish to bring my experience working in a fast-paced environment, working with other like-minded individuals whom I can trust, rely on and learn from so that we can create the best possible apps that will positively impact others. All in all, arts and programming allow me to put my utmost dedication into the craft and create the best possible works through my own abilities.
